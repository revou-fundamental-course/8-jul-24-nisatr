BMI Calculator

BMI, or body mass index, measures your total body fat based on your weight and height. It gives you a clue to your risk for weight-related health problems. Let's check your BMI by filling out this form below!

What this calculator can do:

  1. Calculates body weight
  2. Determines the ideal weight category or not
  3. Preparing a weight loss program

The score you get is valid for most people over age 18 with the exceptions of overestimating body fat in those with a muscular build like an athlete and underestimating fat in those who have lost muscle.

This calculator will give you an idea of how your weight compares to typical values. Body mass index (BMI) is calculated by your weight (Kg) divided by the square of your height (M), or BMI = Kg/M2.
